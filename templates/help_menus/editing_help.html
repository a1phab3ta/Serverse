Help: Editing a Partner

Step 1: Click on the Partner's Name

Start by locating the partner you want to edit in the list of partners. Click on the partner's name to access their details.

Step 2: Update Information

Once you're on the partner's details page, edit all the rows you want to change.

Step 3: Submit

Once you are done changing all of the rows that you want, click the Submit button.

Step 4: Confirm

An alert will pop up at the top of your screen asking you to confirm the edit. Click "OK" to confirm.
