Help: Adding a Partner

Step 1: Click on the "Add New Partner" Button

Start by navigating to the main page of the partner database application. Locate and click on the "Add New Partner" button.

Step 2: Fill out the Partner Information Form

Once you've clicked on the button, you'll be directed to a form where you can enter the details of the new partner. Fill out the form with accurate information.

Step 3: Click on the "Submit" Button to Add the Partner

After filling out the form, review the information to ensure it's correct. Then, click on the "Submit" button to add the partner to the database.
